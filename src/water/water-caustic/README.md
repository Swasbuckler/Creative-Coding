# Water Caustics

This page consists of a port of [Evan Wallace](https://madebyevan.com/)'s WebGL Water Demo to a ThreeJS and React Three Fiber Environment.
Other than porting their render, **I do not claim ownership over this render**.

You can access their Demo of their original work [here](http://madebyevan.com/webgl-water/), along with the related [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/evanw/webgl-water).

## Additional Information

* The Shaders used for this render are from Evan Wallace with minor tweaks applied for them to function in ThreeJS
* This Render is made atop WebGL
